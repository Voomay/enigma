Our Professional Services

We deliver premium-grade solutions tailored for commercial, industrial, and residential setups to guarantee safety, sanitation, and compliance.

Pest Control

Effective control and prevention of pests including rodents, insects, termites, and more for homes, offices, and industries.

Structural Fumigation & Commodity

Eliminates pests in structures, stored products, and commodities through safe and highly effective gas fumigation processes (including Methyl Bromide).

Industrial Fogging

ULV fogging for large facilities and open areas to control flying insects, odours, bacteria, and airborne contaminants.

Beetle Inspection

Professional inspections for wood-destroying beetles with detailed reports and formal Clearance Certificates (COC) for real estate transfers.

Soil Poisoning

Pre-construction soil chemical treatments to prevent future termite infestations and protect the structural foundation of your investment.

Termite Control

Advanced termite management and eradication solutions, including chemical barriers, active baiting, and monitoring systems.

Electrical COC

Official Electrical Certificate of Compliance (COC) testing and issuing by qualified professionals to ensure regulatory legality.

Plumbing COC

Plumbing Certificate of Compliance inspections ensuring water, sewage, and heating installations are safe and meet building codes.

Hygiene & Sanitation

Deep cleaning, disinfection, and sanitation products and services that promote health, safety, and a cleaner operating environment.

Housekeeping

Tailored, professional corporate, industrial, and household housekeeping and maintenance contracts.

About Our Company

Enigma Integrated Solutions

Enigma Integrated Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a premier South African compliance and pest eradication firm. We specialize in bringing property owners absolute peace of mind through a combined suite of professional pest control, structural gas fumigations, and certified building compliance audits.

Our qualified teams operate strictly within national health and safety directives, issuing legally binding Certificates of Compliance (COC) for electrical installations, plumbing systems, and wood borer beetle clearances. We protect your investments, sanitize your workspaces, and guarantee complete regulatory compliance.
