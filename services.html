Pest Control

Enigma Integrated Solutions provides premium, customized pest control programs designed to protect residential homes, corporate offices, and heavy industrial facilities from pest intrusion. We specialize in the complete eradication and ongoing management of crawling insects, rodents, cockroaches, flies, and structural pests.

Our technicians use SANS-approved formulations and follow strict safety protocols. By utilizing advanced baiting stations, targeted spray barriers, and pheromone tracking systems, we stop infestations before they impact your business operations or family health.

Book Inspection

Structural Fumigation & Commodity

For deep-seated infestations such as wood-borer beetles or stored product pests inside silos, warehouses, and domestic properties, standard spraying is insufficient. Enigma is fully licensed to conduct large-scale, gas-tight structural fumigations using SANS-compliant Methyl Bromide.

We manage the entire process: property tarping, pressure sealing, safety perimeter monitoring with gas detection scales, regulatory warning notices, and final safe aeration. Our procedures are highly audited and fully certified.

Request Fumigation Quote

Industrial Fogging

Eradicating flying insects, airborne bacteria, and contaminants in large warehouses, agricultural setups, and commercial offices requires specialized fogging. We deploy ULV (Ultra-Low Volume) fogging machines that disperse microscopic droplets of sanitization compounds and eco-safe insecticides.

This mist penetrates hard-to-reach cracks, ceilings, and ventilation shafts, ensuring absolute coverage and rapid disinfection. Fogging is perfect for deep-cleaning operations and odor neutralization.

Schedule Fogging

Beetle Inspection

Wood-boring beetles can silently destroy structural beams, roofing timbers, and floorboards, devaluing your property. Enigma's certified wood inspectors conduct thorough audits of timber frameworks, roofing structures, and floor plates to spot active infestations.

Following SANS directives, we supply official Beetle Clearance Certificates (COC) essential for South African real estate property transfers, protecting both buyers and sellers from hidden liabilities.

Book Beetle Inspection

Soil Poisoning

Subterranean termites pose a constant threat to foundations. Our pre-construction soil poisoning involves treating the soil beneath floor slabs and around foundation footings with specialized chemical barriers before concrete is poured.

Operating strictly under SANS 10018, this chemical treatment blocks termite entry, protecting the structural integrity of your building projects for years to come. We supply legally compliant certification upon completion.

Request Soil Poisoning Quote

Termite Control

For existing properties suffering active termite damage, Enigma delivers advanced management solutions. We install sub-slab chemical injection barriers, perimeter trenches, and active monitoring/baiting stations.

Our baiting systems use slow-acting growth regulators that termites carry back to their colony, eliminating the queen and resolving the infestation at the source without heavy structural disruption.

Schedule Termite Audit

Electrical COC

Legislation requires property owners to hold a valid Electrical Certificate of Compliance (COC) for transfers or safety audits. Our certified compliance inspectors audit your entire installation.

We check distribution boards, circuit breakers, grounding links, plugs, and switches to ensure everything is SANS compliant and legal, issuing COCs or detailing necessary remediation work.

Book Electrical Audit

Plumbing COC

Leaky geysers, sub-standard drainage lines, and poor water connections pose major property hazards. Our plumbing inspectors conduct detailed audits of incoming and outgoing pipelines, water heaters, valves, and sewer links.

We ensure all plumbing installations meet municipality regulations and South African building standards, issuing formal Plumbing Certificates of Compliance (COC).

Book Plumbing Audit
